RULES AND REGULATION OF NCC

DEFINITION

  • "Governing Council" means the Governing Council of NCCAA.
  • "Executive Committee" means the Executive Committee of NCCAA.
  • "President" means the President of Governing Council of NCCAA
  • "Chairman" means the Chairman of Executive Committee of NCCAA.
  • "Secretary" means the Secretary of NCCAA
  • "Treasurer" means the Treasurer of NCCAA

    • AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

  • An Association whose primary aim is to bring NCC alumni together for furtherance of aims of NCC and assist in Nation building.
  • To foster and strengthen the affinity between the alumni of the NCC, India.
  • To execute programmes in consonance with the above object and undertake such activities and through such means that promotes the object of enhancement of standards.
  • To get valuable advice and assist NCC in maintaining a very high standard of training and sports
  • To maintain and update the database of registered Alumni with NCCAA.
  • To utilise experience of registered Alumni for benefit and progress of enrolled NCC cadets.
